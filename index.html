Our Story

A Home on the Edge of the Hill

Perched on the edge of Manipal hill, Parnakuti Homestay offers a rare glimpse of the Arabian Sea shimmering on the horizon. It's not just a place to sleep — it's a world that slows down for you.

Owned with heart and run with warmth, Parnakuti is a pet-friendly, family-friendly retreat where pets, birds, and nature greet you every morning. Our host Uday brings local wisdom, insider tips, and genuine care to every stay.
